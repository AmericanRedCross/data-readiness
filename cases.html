Community Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness Program (CP3)

The goal of CP3 is to strengthen the ability of communities and civil society groups in selected countries to prevent, detect, and respond to disease threats and play a central and significant role in preparing for future risks.

Working with organisation and government agencies, CP3 accesses datasets in their custody and encourages them to put data into the Humanitarian Data Exchange. Where data is not available, CP3 conducts field data collection activities to create data layers that can be used by the National Society and other stakeholders in responding to emergencies, including markets data, health facilities & clinics, schools, community locations and details, places of worship, and administrative boundaries defined by communities.
