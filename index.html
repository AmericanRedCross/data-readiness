An overview of data readiness

In the Red Cross Red Crescent Movement context, data readiness is the ability of National Societies to use quality and timely information to influence operations and planning to respond effectively to disasters.

Why it matters

The IFRC and National Societies are thinking more critically about data and how they can leverage it better to improve our work. Through data readiness, organizations will be equipped to use quality and timely information to influence operations and planning, all with the goal of improving our ability to respond effectively to disasters.

How to get there

There are many paths to becoming a data ready organization, but this toolkit lays out an easy-to-follow roadmap for data readiness for IFRC and National Societies. Whether you're learning about data readiness or the first time, developing a strategy for your team, or putting together a project proposal this toolkit can help you meet those goals.
